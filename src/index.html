About Us

As a maritime country with over 17,000 islands, Indonesia demands strong logistics at sea. PT Dian Unggul Samudra Perkasa was established to support this need by providing tugboats and barges, mainly for coal transshipment and infrastructure transport across Sumatra, Java, Kalimantan, and Sulawesi.

Our Services

  • Shipping Business – Tugboat and Barge Rental (Time Charter & Freight Charter)
  • Docking Services – Shipyard Facilities
  • Shipping Agency – Port and Vessel Operations

Our Fleet

We operate a diverse fleet including tugboats such as DWI PUTRA 01, ANTASENA, and LUMBA-LUMBA, and barges like ESA VIII and BAIDURI 30256, to serve various project needs.
